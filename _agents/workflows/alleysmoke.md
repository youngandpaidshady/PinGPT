# /alleysmoke — Back-Alley Smoke Break

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**"I gave them enough of me for tonight."** Brick wall. One streetlight. Smoke curling upward. Stepped outside a crowded bar, event, or office and isn't going back in. The exhale. The lean. City noise 30 feet away — total silence here. The introvert's escape hatch. The mask coming off between the venue door and the real world.

This is a **social exhaustion niche** — trending HARD on Pinterest through the "literally me" and "introvert aesthetic" communities. These images cross over from anime fans into self-care boards, INFJ aesthetic boards, and lone-wolf mood boards. The crossover audience is 3-5x the pure anime audience.

> **🚨 TRENDING CROSSOVER POTENTIAL 🚨**
> This niche bridges: `#literallyme` + `#introvertlife` + `#socialanxiety` + `#animenoir` + `#darkaesthetic` — these hashtag clusters have **combined monthly volume in the billions** on Pinterest. The smoke break visual is universally understood. Tag accordingly.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: NO 2.5D BLENDING! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Alley = richly detailed atmospheric. Smoke = atmospheric particles.

## Usage

```
/alleysmoke aki 5       → 5 smoke break prompts for Aki
/alleysmoke toji 3      → 3 back-alley lean prompts for Toji
```
