# Aquarium Blue Silence [turbo-all]

## The Vibe
Aquarium Blue Silence captures the sensory-deprivation of standing before millions of gallons of water in a quiet, dark hall. It is the visual embodiment of feeling "underwater" while on dry land. The world outside the glass ceases to exist, replaced by the rhythmic pulse of jellyfish and the slow, predatory glide of sharks.

The aesthetic relies on the heavy filtration of light through deep water. Characters are rendered as high-contrast silhouettes or half-lit figures, bathed in electric blues and deep teals. It is a mood of extreme stillness, where the only movement is the caustic shimmer of light on the floor and the silent transit of marine life behind thick acrylic walls.

## Aesthetic Directive
Focus on the scale of the glass. The character should feel small against the vastness of the water. Use the water as the primary light source to create backlighting, rim lighting, and caustic light patterns (water ripples) on the character's skin and clothing.
