# /edgeofbed — Sitting Up at 3AM

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**He woke up. He shouldn't have.** 3AM. Sitting on the edge of the bed. Bare back. Sheets pooled at the waist. Room pitch black except for blinds cutting the city light into stripes across his body. Not a nightmare — worse. Awake. Mind running. The weight of everything he carried during the day landed the moment his head hit the pillow, and now he's sitting up because lying down feels like drowning.

This is a **weight niche** — the image of a muscular/strong character sitting on the bed edge with their back to the camera is one of the most SAVED compositions in anime aesthetic Pinterest. It works because: (1) the back-to-camera creates mystery, (2) the muscular back in vulnerability creates dissonance, (3) EVERYONE has been this person at 3AM, sitting up because sleep rejected them. The audience doesn't scroll past this image. They stop. They save. They've been here.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: NO 2.5D BLENDING! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Bedroom = richly detailed atmospheric. Light through blinds = atmospheric.

## Usage

```
/edgeofbed toji 5      → 5 edge-of-bed prompts for Toji
/edgeofbed any 8       → 8 prompts rotating characters
/edgeofbed             → 3 prompts, auto-picks
```
