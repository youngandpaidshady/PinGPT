# /gallerystill — Museum & Gallery Stillness

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Alone with art you can't afford and silence you can't buy.** The echo of footsteps on marble. The hush that makes you whisper even when you're alone. A character standing in front of a painting larger than themselves, and for the first time all day, they're not performing. The gallery is the only place the world stops moving long enough to feel something.

This is the **gallery stillness** niche — characters in empty museums, art galleries, exhibition spaces after closing. The audience here is the intersection of anime aesthetic and art/design culture — mood board curators, interior design inspiration seekers, and the "art hoe" aesthetic community.

/gallerystill nanami 3  → 3 museum-contemplation prompts for Nanami

## Anti-Repetition (batch-level)

- **No two prompts** may use the same gallery/museum type
- **No two prompts** may use the same pose
- **No two prompts** may use the same emotional vibe
- **At least 1** must be an after-hours/near-dark scene (security lighting only)
- **At least 1** must feature the character visibly affected by the art (expression change, stopped mid-step)
- **At least 1** must use the reflection-in-glass composition (character seeing themselves overlaid on art)
