# /jerseycore — Sports Streetwear Anime Aesthetic

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**He doesn't play. He IS the team.** A character stepping off the field at golden hour, jersey stretched across broad shoulders, ball tucked under one arm, city skyline behind him. The confidence of an athlete who doesn't need to prove anything. Jersey Core is where anime fandom meets sports identity meets streetwear culture — characters in football kits, basketball tanks, and athletic compression wear, styled like editorial shoots.

This is the **fastest-rising anime aesthetic on Pinterest and TikTok in 2025-2026.** The audience isn't just anime fans — it's the streetwear × sports × anime triple-overlap that drives the highest engagement per pin. Blue Lock characters practically live here, but ANY character in an athlete's fit hits different.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: TEAM IDENTITY IS THE WHOLE POINT 🚨**
> Unlike storefronts and signage (where NB2 hallucinates random kanji), jersey numbers and team crests are VISUAL PATTERNS the model handles well. Describe specific teams, badge colors, and kit numbers directly. The jersey IS the content — lean into real team identity. "Wearing a Brazil national team yellow jersey with green trim and number 10" is EXACTLY the prompt energy we want.

## 🔥 Assembly Formula (MANDATORY)

> Every prompt MUST follow this exact construction order. The DNA tables above are your ingredient pools — this section is HOW you assemble them.

**Step 1 — CHARACTER + 2-3 physical traits** (front-loaded for AI recognition)

**Step 2 — SPATIAL ANCHOR with athletic narrative**
- ❌ "stands on a football field" (static backdrop)
- ✅ "alone on a floodlit training pitch after everyone else left an hour ago" (frozen moment in a story)

**Step 3 — JERSEY as identity (2-3 beats of kit description)**
- ❌ "blue jersey" (nothing)
- ✅ "sleek dark navy jersey with white collar trim, fabric slightly damp from practice, sleeves pushed past elbows" (identity + story)

**Step 4 — PROP HIERARCHY (3 anchors at different scales)**
- Macro: pitch, court, tunnel, railing, bench
- Mid: ball under arm, jacket over jersey, water bottle in hand
- Micro: grass stain at knee, tape on wrist, collar perfectly folded

**Step 5 — FUSED POSE-EMOTION** (one phrase combining body + energy)
- ❌ Pose: "leaning against wall" + Vibe: "Main Character Energy"
- ✅ "leaning against concrete tunnel wall with the patient composure of someone who has already decided the outcome"

**Step 6 — NAMED LIGHT SOURCE with direction**
- ❌ "golden lighting, warm atmosphere"
- ✅ "sharp golden sunset light from behind-left, painting the jersey gold, while cool blue building shadow fills the right"

**Step 7 — SHADOW COLOR mapped to jersey/character palette**

**Step 8 — BINARY PALETTE as contrast pair**
- ✅ "Navy jersey against amber sunset palette"
- ✅ "White kit against deep charcoal urban palette"

**Step 9 — STANDARD NB2 SAFETY SUFFIX**
- End with the standard anti-text/watermark line: "NO kanji, NO watermarks, NO signatures, clean image."
- Do NOT ban jersey numbers or crests — they are visual elements, not text artifacts.

### Quick Self-Check Before Finalizing

| # | Check | If NO... |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Does the spatial anchor include an athletic narrative phrase? | Add "after/before/during [match/practice/selection]" |
| 2 | Is the jersey described with 2-3 beats including fabric state? | Add color + collar + condition detail |
| 3 | Are there 3 props at macro/mid/micro scale? | Add the missing scale layer |
| 4 | Is the pose fused with athletic-emotional intent? | Merge pose and vibe into single phrase |
| 5 | Does the jersey description include specific team identity (colors, crest, number)? | Add team kit details — that's the whole appeal |
