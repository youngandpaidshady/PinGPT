# /late_shift — The Exhausted Return

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**The day is finally over.** The character has just walked through the front door of their apartment after a grueling day (or a walk in the rain). The setting highlights the transition from the cold, harsh outside world to the warm, messy sanctuary of home. 

This mood is deeply relatable. It features loosened ties, dropped bags, discarded shoes, and coffee tables cluttered with instant ramen and papers. The lighting is crucial: harsh clinical blue or rainy blue light from the hallway/window clashing against the cozy, warm yellow glow of a solitary desk lamp inside the room.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: EXHAUSTION & MESSY SANCTUARIES! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = highly detailed cluttered room interiors with extreme warm/cool lighting split.

## Usage
