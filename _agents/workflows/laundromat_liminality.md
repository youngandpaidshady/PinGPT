# Laundromat Liminality (/laundry) [turbo-all]

## The Vibe

There is a specific kind of peace found only at 3:00 AM between the rinse and spin cycles. The world outside is dark and demanding, but inside the glass storefront, time is measured by the rhythmic thumping of heavy denim against steel drums. It is a purgatory of productivity—a place where you are forced to wait, trapped in a bubble of fluorescent light and the scent of synthetic lavender.

The aesthetic thrives on the contrast between the sterile and the soft. Hard plastic molded chairs meet oversized, worn-out hoodies. The harsh, flickering hum of ceiling lights clashes with the warm, amber glow emanating from the belly of a dryer. It is the visual representation of "loading," a transition state where the protagonist is neither where they were nor where they are going, simply existing in the hum.

## Aesthetic Directive
**The Rule of Circularity & Steam:** Every composition must prioritize the circular geometry of the machine drums and the distortive quality of steam-fogged glass. Reflections of the character must appear warped in the chrome or glass surfaces, emphasizing the feeling of being "suspended" in a cycle.

## Scene Film Strip — Viral Assembly Formula

1. **Setting the Frame:** Establish the laundromat interior (e.g., Row of glowing washers).
2. **The Subject:** Place the character in the designated Outfit Lock (e.g., Oversized hoodie).
3. **The Action (No Emotion):** Apply a Pose Lock focusing on physical evidence (e.g., Forehead pressed against glass).
4. **Primary Light:** Define the overhead fluorescent source (Cool white).
5. **Secondary Light:** Define the machine source (Warm orange/amber glow).
6. **Micro-Detail:** Add a distortion element (e.g., Warped reflection in spinning chrome).
7. **Palette Check:** Ensure 40% Detergent Blue and 25% Plastic Orange balance.
8. **Render Style:** MIXED MEDIA: flat 2D anime cel-shaded character on [environment in 2-3 words]. NO text, NO watermarks, clean image.
9. **Final Polish:** Remove all emotion words (sad, lonely, bored). Replace with "drooping shoulders," "hollow stare," and "rhythmic thumping."

## Anti-Repetition
- Ensure no two scenes use the same Machine Type (alternate top-load vs. front-load).
- Flip-flop between "Inside the store looking out" and "Aisle perspective."
- Rotate through all 10 Outfit Locks to avoid "Grey Hoodie Syndrome."
- If the previous image was Blue-dominant, the next must be Orange-dominant.
