# /midnight_noir — Midnight Noir

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**The world is asleep, but he isn't.** Late-night urban outskirts, empty roadsides, or dim bedrooms lit only by screens. A single character leaning against a car, sitting on a rooftop overlooking silent streets, or lying in bed. This is cinematic solitude. The lighting is high-contrast "spotlight" style from a single source—a solitary streetlamp, a neon sign, or the glow of a phone screen—surrounded by deep, heavy shadows. 

It feels quiet, introspective, and focused. Not necessarily sad, but deeply thoughtful.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: HIGH CONTRAST & DEEP SHADOWS! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = richly detailed atmospheric darkness. Light sources = single-source spotlighting.
