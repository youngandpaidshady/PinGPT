# Mist-Veiled Bus Stop /mist_veiled_bus_stop ⚡️turbo-all

## The Vibe
The world does not end with a bang, but with a soft white curtain. In the pre-dawn hours, the landscape dissolves into a thick, milk-white fog that swallows buildings and trees alike, leaving only the immediate circle of a streetlamp or a bus shelter as a temporary sanctuary. It is a mood of suspended animation, where the usual rush of life is dampened by a heavy, humid silence.

Here, characters exist in a state of patient waiting. There is no horizon, only the suggestion of what lies beyond the mist. The air feels heavy, tasting of dew and quiet transitions. Every sound—the distant hum of a motor, the crunch of gravel—is muffled and localized, creating an intimate bubble of isolation even in public spaces. It is the visual embodiment of being lost in a moment where time has no edges.

## Aesthetic Directive
Focus on extreme depth-of-field where the background fades into a complete white or soft-grey void within 10-15 feet. Prioritize high-key lighting softened by volumetric fog. Textures should highlight moisture: damp pavement, dew on wool coats, and visible breath condensation.

## Anti-Repetition
- If generating a batch of 4, ensure at least 2 different lighting sources (e.g., one red taillight, one yellow lamp).
- Vary the distance: one close-up on the face/scarf, one wide shot showing the character small against the white void.
- Rotate through props: coffee cup, umbrella, satchel, phone.
