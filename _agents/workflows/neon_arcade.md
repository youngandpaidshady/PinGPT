# /neon_arcade — Neon Arcade

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Alone in a crowd of noise and light.** Surrounded by the bright, chaotic glow of Japanese arcade cabinets, claw machines, and coin-pushers. The environment is visually overwhelming, flashing with pinks, greens, and blues, but the character remains completely disconnected and isolated, lost in their own world.

The contrast here is key: vibrant, loud, and social surroundings clashing with the character's internal quietness and solitary nature.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: CHAOTIC NEON & REFLECTIONS! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = highly detailed retro arcade elements, screen glares, and vibrant contrasting light sources.

## Usage

```

/neon_arcade any 8         
/neon_arcade
```
