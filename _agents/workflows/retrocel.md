# /retrocel — Nostalgic Cel-Shade Aesthetic

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Before digital. Before perfection. When anime was paint on celluloid.** The VHS tracking lines, the slightly misregistered colors, the warm grain of a CRT monitor at midnight — this isn't retro for retro's sake. It's the aesthetic of an era when anime felt handmade, when every frame was a physical painting, and the imperfections were the beauty.

This is the **nostalgic cel-shade** niche — 90s-era flat color fills, analog warmth, Cowboy Bebop sunsets and Evangelion blues. The audience here isn't just anime fans — they're design-conscious millennials and Gen-Z discovering the beauty of analog media. These images live on mood boards, desktop wallpapers, and design inspiration feeds.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: NO MODERN RENDERING! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading with VISIBLE cel-paint imperfections (slight color bleed at edges, subtle cell overlay seams). Background = warm photographic grain, NOT clean digital bokeh. Force the ANALOG feel. No smooth digital gradients — stepped color fills only.

## Usage

```
/retrocel gojo 5       → 5 retro cel-shade prompts for Gojo
/retrocel spike 3      → 3 prompts for Spike in full 90s aesthetic
```

## Visual Style Adaptation (CRITICAL — differs from standard PinGPT)

> **Retrocel modifies the standard Visual Style Lock from `skill.md`:**

| Standard PinGPT | Retrocel Override |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| richly detailed atmospheric background with heavy bokeh | **Warm atmospheric background with visible film grain, NO digital bokeh — use shallow depth of field with grain** |
| Grainy film texture | **HEAVY grain — specify "35mm film grain" or "VHS grain texture" — the grain is a feature, not a finish** |
| Clean cel-shading | **Cel-shading with slight imperfections — "visible cel-paint edges, subtle warm color bleed between fills"** |
| Muted/desaturated default | **Warm and slightly oversaturated — push amber/gold tones, avoid cool desaturation** |
| Neon Bleed palette option | **BANNED for retrocel — neon is a modern/cyberpunk signal. Replace with warm incandescent, paper lanterns, and sunset light** |
