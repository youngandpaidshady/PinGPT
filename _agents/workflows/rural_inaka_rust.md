# /rural_inaka_rust 🎋 [turbo-all]

### The Vibe

Rural Inaka Rust is the aesthetic of a town that has stopped breathing while the sun continues to bake it. It is the visual embodiment of high-noon humidity in rural Japan—the kind that makes the air shimmer and the cicadas deafening. Unlike the clean, romanticized countryside, this mood focuses on the "Inaka-core" reality: rusted corrugated metal, sun-bleached plastics, and the aggressive encroachment of tall weeds on man-made structures.

There is a heavy, stagnant weight to these scenes. Characters are caught in the transition between leaving and staying, standing under power lines that hum with electricity nobody seems to use. It is nostalgic not because it is beautiful, but because it is persistent. The palette is dominated by the harsh contrast of zinc-white sunlight against deep, saturated greens and the textured orange of oxidizing iron.

### Aesthetic Directive
Prioritize harsh, vertical sunlight and high-contrast shadows. Every metal surface must show signs of weathering, rust, or oxidation. Foliage should look overgrown and unkempt, pressing against fences and concrete. The air should look "thick" through the use of subtle heat haze and dust particles.

### Usage
- `/mood:rural_inaka_rust <character>`
- `/mood:rural_inaka_rust <character> standing by a train crossing`
- `/mood:rural_inaka_rust <character> near overgrown vending machines`

### Steps
1. **Read Skills:** Internalize the physical markers of rural stagnation (rust, weeds, high sun).
2. **Parse Input:** Identify the character and any specific rural environment requested.
