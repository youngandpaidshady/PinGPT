# Shallow Mirror-Tide [turbo-all]

## The Vibe
Shallow Mirror-Tide is the visual manifestation of a "boundless exhale." It draws inspiration from the surreal, horizon-less landscapes of flooded salt flats and low-tide shorelines at first light. The character is not just standing in water; they are suspended in a world where the distinction between heaven and earth has dissolved. The water acts as a perfect, unmoving mirror, duplicating the vast, cloud-heavy sky beneath the character's feet.

The mood is one of ethereal clarity. It represents a moment of absolute mental stillness—a "dream-core" sanctuary where the weight of the world is replaced by the buoyancy of light and reflection. It is quiet, majestic, and visually vast, focusing on the surreal symmetry of a single figure caught in an infinite blue loop.

## Aesthetic Directive
**Horizon Erasure.** Every composition must emphasize the seamless blend of the sky and its reflection. The floor is a mirror; the character is the only anchor between two identical heavens.
