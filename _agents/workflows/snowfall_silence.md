# /snowfall_silence — Snowfall Silence

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**The world is muffled by the cold.** Solitary figures standing in falling snow, watching the flakes descend. Deep winter coats, scarves hiding the lower half of the face, and visible breath in the freezing air. The defining characteristic is the profound, insulating quiet of a snowy night or overcast winter day. 

The vibe is peaceful isolation. The cold outside makes the small warmth inside (a hot coffee, a thick scarf) feel monumental.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: MUFFLED ATMOSPHERE AND FROST! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = soft, diffused light, atmospheric snow particles, and muted contrast.
