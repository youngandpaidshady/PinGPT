# /stormy_solace — Stormy Solace

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Chaos outside, stillness inside.** Rural roadside stalls, jungle bridges, or bus stops during a violent storm. Features wet pavement, rain puddles, lush foliage bending in the wind, and atmospheric mist. The lighting is overcast and desaturated, punctuated by the sudden flash of lightning or the soft, hazy glow of a warm lantern.

The vibe is intense but incredibly cozy (a "hygge" in the storm). It emphasizes the stark contrast between the chaotic weather outside and the quiet stillness of the character seeking shelter.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: TEXTURED RAIN & WET SURFACES! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = highly detailed wet surfaces, reflections, and atmospheric mist.
