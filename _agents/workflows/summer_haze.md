# /summer_haze — Summer Haze

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Blistering heat, blinding light, and the hum of cicadas.** The quintessential Japanese summer aesthetic. Intense, overexposed sunlight casting sharp, deep shadows. Sitting in the shade of a shrine, sprawling on a tatami mat next to a fan, or standing under a giant cumulonimbus cloud holding a melting ice pop or condensation-covered Ramune bottle.

The vibe is deeply nostalgic, lethargic, and blindingly bright. It evokes the feeling of childhood summers that stretch on forever, now viewed through the melancholic lens of an isolated present.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: OVEREXPOSED LIGHT & SHARP SHADOWS! 🚨**
> Character = flat 2D anime cel-shading. Environment = bright, saturated, sun-bleached colors with pitch-black shadows.
