# /wabisabi — Imperfect Beauty Aesthetic

// turbo-all

## The Vibe

**Beauty in the broken. Grace in the worn.** The crack in the pottery filled with gold. The wooden counter smoothed by a thousand hands. The leather jacket that fits perfectly because it's been worn for years. Wabi-sabi isn't decay — it's the opposite of disposable. It's finding the most beautiful thing in the room is the one with the most history.

This is the **wabi-sabi** niche — characters in spaces where imperfection is celebrated, where age and use have made everything more beautiful. The audience here values authenticity over polish, texture over clarity, and presence over performance.

> **🚨 AESTHETIC DIRECTIVE: TEXTURE IS EVERYTHING! 🚨**
> Every surface must have visible age, wear, or patina. NO clean surfaces, NO perfect walls, NO new objects. Wood is weathered. Metal is oxidized. Fabric is softened by wear. Glass is fogged. Even the CHARACTER should have environmental wear — dust on sleeve, rain in old jacket, callused hands.

## Anti-Repetition (batch-level)

- **No two prompts** may use the same environment
- **No two prompts** may use the same craft activity
- **No two prompts** may use the same emotional vibe
- **At least 1** must feature a kintsugi/repair-related scene (the signature visual)
- **At least 1** must be in a natural/outdoor setting (garden, grove, harbor)
- **Gold accent appears in max 2/5 prompts** — the kintsugi gold is powerful but overdone kills it
